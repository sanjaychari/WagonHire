OVERVIEW

Have you ever travelled out of the country or out of your city for over a year


and wondered about the state of your vehicle back at home? Most of the time, it


would just sit there gathering and collecting dust. Did you ever feel like you


could've made some valuable money by renting it out to another person?


You are new to the city and feel like you are spending too much on autorickshaw


fares. However, you cannot afford to buy your own vehicle. Your post here lasts


only for three months. Wouldn't it be most convenient for you to rent a vehicle?


If your answer to any of the above was yes, then WagonHire is the answer for you.


WagonHire efficiently matches vehicle owners wishing to rent out their vehicles to


potential renters. With WagonHire, your worrying days are over.



IMPLEMENTATION

The website takes input information from every user (renter and rentee) on the website.


There exist two databases on the back end; a renter database and a rentee database.


For every renter or rentee, the website scans through the other database for users


whose details match the given renter or rentee.



BUSINESS MODEL

The rentee of a vehicle puts up a monthly price for his/her vehicle. The renter can then


try to bargain or accept the given price. Once the deal between the renter and rentee is


finalised, WagonHire charges an additional 15% monthly convenience fee on the rentee.



Disclaimer : Any damage to the vehicle is to be tended to by the renter before


returning the vehicle. All damage to the vehicle is the responsibility of the renter.



PRIVACY

All information supplied by the user on the website is for interaction purposes only.


WagonHire is a completely clean interface and does not use the existing databases for


for any purpose apart from matching renters and rentees.
